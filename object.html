OUR OBJECTIVES  

 

1. To promote literacy within the community by providing sustainable structures, resources, connections, and communication.

 

2. To offer literacy support for adult members of the community whose literacy levels are currently hindering their ability to fully participate in daily life at home, work, and in the community.

 

3. To provide free supported computer access to the community.

 

4. To create opportunities for community celebrations centred on and around literacy.

 

5. To encourage and promote family literacy within our community.
